Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Eva transports company was brought about by a trio who saw the need of combining public and private transportation for greater efficiency. The company has become an icon in the transportation industry widely spreading its routes across six main states in the country.

We are a reliable transportation company providing safe, comfortable and private rides for individuals, groups and businesses. With years of experience and a passion for excellence, we ensure every journey is smooth and enjoyable.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional service with professional drivers, affordable pricing and 24/7 customer support.
